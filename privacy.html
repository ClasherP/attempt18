Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-09-01

Overview

Dictate Chess does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data from users.

Data Collection

  • No account information is collected.
  • No browsing history, page contents, or keystrokes are collected.
  • No analytics or tracking technologies are used.
  • No data is sent to third parties or external servers.

Local Functionality Only

All functionality runs locally in your browser. Any temporary state (e.g., on/off settings) may be stored using Chrome’s local/sync storage solely to remember your preferences on your device. This storage is not transmitted to the developer or to third parties.

Permissions

The Extension may request Chrome permissions strictly to provide its features (e.g., access to the active tab to interact with the page). These permissions do not grant the developer access to your personal data and are not used to collect information.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this page with an updated effective date.
